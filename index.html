What is LibreFortune?

LibreFortune is a revolutionary redesign of Bitcoin that aims to standardize the new generation of digital decentralized currency.

Features

  1. Proof of Fortune
  2. Green Mining
  3. More powerful and extensible payment scheme
  4. Capital risk-free pooled mining
  5. Secure thin client
  6. Upgradable Protocol
  7. And much more ...
